Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit orginization that is run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowed, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain audience! As one of our members told us: "Each year I give a ticket package to each of our family members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge...and that is priceless>"
